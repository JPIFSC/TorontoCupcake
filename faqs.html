Frequently Asked Questions

ORDERING

It is helpful to give a minimum of 48 hours notice for custom orders.

The minimum order is 1/2 dozen cupcakes.

You need to place your order before 5PM Toronto Canada time to have it delievered the following day.

Once we recieve your order we check it for errors or omissions and then send you an invoice via Paypal. You can the click on the link provided and pay using paypal or most major credit cards. We can also accept cheques or interac e transfers (Canada only).

We accept Visa MasterCard AmericanExpress and other major cards. We also accept Debit cards.

Payment is due when you recieve your invoice via email.

DELIVERY

Delivery fees vary based on your location and range from $15 - $35 CAD. Free delivery is available for large orders.

Our earliest delivery window is 8am to noon. Our latest delivery window is noon to 6pm, 7 days a week. If you need something outside of this delivery schedule please call or email us and we will try to accommodate.

If you are planning on eating them within a day you can store them at room temperature. If you are not going to eat them right away you can freeze them. To thaw them, take them out of the freezer and cover them lightly with plastic wrap while they thaw. This will minimize condensation forming on the cupcakes. They should be ready to eat in about an hour.
You should not refrigerate your cupcakes overnight because they will dry out.
